Core library, where all the goodies live.

Sensei Gateways

Hadoop indexer

Map-reduce job to build indexes from Hadoop.

Depends on Sensei Core.

<dependency>
  <groupId>com.senseidb</groupId>
  <artifactId>sensei-hadoop-indexing</artifactId>
  <version>{{page.version}}</version>
</depedency>

War packaging

For people who want to deploy Sensei in a container, we provide a war packaging for Sensei.

Sensei configuration are read from the 

sensei-conf
directory.

Depends on Sensei Core.

<dependency>
  <groupId>com.senseidb</groupId>
  <artifactId>sensei-war</artifactId>
  <version>{{page.version}}</version>
</depedency>
