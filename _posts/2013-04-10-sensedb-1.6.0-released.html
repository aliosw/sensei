We are excited to release SenseiDB 1.6.0. Since release 1.5.0, we have gained a new committer: Yonghui Zhao from Xiaomi Inc. This release is a joint effort from teams from LinkedIn, Twitter and Xiaomi.

The theme of this release is big data analytics. We are seeing many instances of Sensei deployed as a big data analytics tool, so we beefed up BQL to support advanced analytics operators, e.g. sum, avg, distinctCount etc. For more information, see here.

We also cleaned up our backlog of bugs. See details here.
